About Us

your legal partner & geodetic engineer

Repol Law Office and Land Surveying Services is led by Atty. Mary Jane Repol, a skilled geodetic engineer and bar passer. Her journey began with a deep-rooted passion for land surveying, nurtured by her father, Engr. Daniel Yonzon, a civil engineer and real estate broker who founded Yonzon Realty and Surveying.

Together, they ventured into Landlimits, a land surveying company where Atty. Mary Jane managed operations. Her recent admission to the bar prompted the creation of Repol Law Office, combining legal knowledge with geodetic expertise.

At Repol Law Office, we use our proficiency in legal and geodetic solutions to safeguard your land rights.

Services

LAND SURVEYING SERVICES

Discover our core land surveying services, where we provide expert guidance and support for all your land and property needs.

Learn More

Lot Plan with Vicinity

Preparation of a detailed survey plan that shows the exact boundaries and dimensions of a specific land parcel, along with its surrounding features such as roads, buildings, and landmarks.

Relocation Survey

We offer relocation services to assist individuals or businesses with moving their properties to a new location. Our team utilizes advanced surveying techniques to ensure that the relocation process is accurate, efficient, and meets all legal requirements.

Topographic Survey

A topographic survey provides precise land measurements and a comprehensive picture of the layout of an existing site, which can be used to make decisions on the design of drainage systems, the placement of new structures, and the repositioning of existing borders.
